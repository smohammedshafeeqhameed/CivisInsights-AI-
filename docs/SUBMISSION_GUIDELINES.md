# AI Solution Submission Guidelines

This document outlines the submission requirements, evaluation criteria, and other specifics for the AI solution challenge. Please read it carefully to ensure your submission meets all requirements.

## What to Submit

Your submission must include the following three components:

### 1. Clear Proposal

Submit a compelling vision for your AI solution, highlighting its positive impact and alignment with one of the focus areas.

- **Problem Statement**: Clearly articulate the problem your solution addresses and how it will benefit the community.
- **Feasibility & Scalability**: Demonstrate the feasibility of your project and its potential for scalability.

### 2. Functional Prototype

Build a working prototype using Google Cloud's AI tools.

- **Demonstration**: Ensure the prototype effectively demonstrates your solution's capabilities and benefits.

### 3. Comprehensive Documentation

- **Instructions**: Provide detailed instructions for judges to access and test your solution.
- **Video Showcase**: Include a link to a public YouTube or Vimeo video (max 3 minutes) showcasing your AI creation in action.
- **Project Description**: Submit a text description explaining your project's goals, functionalities, technical implementation, and social impact.
- **Category Specification**: Clearly identify the focus area you are submitting for.

---

## Evaluation Criteria

Submissions will be judged based on the following criteria:

### Technical Merit (40%)
- **AI Tool Utilization**: How effectively and creatively did you integrate Google Cloud's AI tools?
- **Coding Expertise**: How well-structured, efficient, and clean is your code?
- **Scalability and Sustainability**: Can your solution be scaled for wider adoption and future improvements?

### User Experience (10%)
- **Intuitive Interface**: Is your solution user-friendly, accessible, and pleasant to interact with?
- **Integration with AI Tools**: How seamless and impactful is the integration of AI Tools into your overall functionality?

### Alignment with Cause (15%)
- **Problem Alignment**: Does your solution perfectly align within the chosen theme?
- **Positive Impact**: Does your solution effectively contribute to the community?

### Innovation & Creativity (20%)
- **Uniqueness and Originality**: Does your solution stand out from the crowd with fresh ideas and a novel approach?
- **Potential for Disruption**: Could your solution significantly change or improve existing processes or industries?

### Market Feasibility (15%)
- **Market Viability**: Is there a large and accessible market for your product, and can it compete effectively?

---

## Submission Specifics

### Functionality
Your project must be functional and demonstrate a clear solution to the chosen social challenge. The solution should be user-friendly, accessible, and scalable.

### Platform Compatibility
Your solution should be compatible with the specified Google Cloud platform and tools. Consider factors like scalability, performance, and integration with existing systems.

### Language
All submission materials, including code, documentation, and presentations, must be in **English**.
